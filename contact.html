Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my restaurant?
Click on the "Add Restaurant" button in the top right corner. You can start with a free Basic account or choose a Premium plan for more visibility.
Is Food Spot free for users?
Yes! Searching for restaurants, reading reviews, and booking tables is 100% free for all foodies.
How do verified reviews work?
To leave a verified review, users must verify their phone number or book a table through our platform. This helps us ensure all feedback is authentic.
