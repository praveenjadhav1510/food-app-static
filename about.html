Who We Are

We built a bigger table.

Food Spot started with a simple frustration: scrolling through thousands of fake reviews just to find a decent slice of pizza. We realized that food isn't about algorithms—it's about connection. So we built a platform that puts chefs, communities, and authentic flavors first.

Our Manifesto

We aren't just a directory. We are a movement against bland food and bad recommendations. Here is what we stand for.

Join the Movement
  • 01. Real Food > Fast Food We prioritize local, independent restaurants over chains.
  • 02. Verification Matters Every review on Food Spot comes from a verified diner.
  • 03. Support Local We take 0% commission on bookings. Your money goes to the chef.
  • 04. Community First Food tastes better when we eat together.

From a Notebook to a Network

In 2023, our founder Alex started keeping a notebook of "hidden gems" in NYC that tourists often missed. Friends started asking for copies. Then friends of friends.

What began as a Google Doc shared between foodies turned into Food Spot—a dedicated platform for discovering the culinary soul of a city. Today, we operate in 12 cities and support over 2,500 local restaurants.

"We wanted to build the Yelp we actually wanted to use."

— Alex Johnson, Founder

Why We Are Different

We do things a little differently around here.

Verified Reviews Only

No bots. No paid fake reviews. Only people who have actually dined there can leave feedback.

Zero Commission

We don't charge restaurants per booking. We believe in fair, transparent flat pricing.

Curated Content

Our "Food Guides" are written by professional food critics and local experts, not algorithms.
