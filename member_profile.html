About Us

We are a family-owned Italian restaurant serving authentic Tuscan cuisine for over 20 years. Our chef uses fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create traditional Italian dishes with modern twists. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner or a family gathering, Luigi's offers the perfect ambiance.

Our pasta is made fresh daily, and our wine cellar features over 100 selections from Italy's finest regions.

Awards: James Beard Nominee 2024, NY Times "Best Pasta" 2023.
