The Best Brunch Spots in NYC: A 2025 Guide

Jessica Chen Dec 1, 2025 • 8 min read
2.4k Views

Brunch is a way of life in New York City. Whether you're looking for classic eggs benedict, fluffy pancakes, or innovative fusion dishes, we've curated the ultimate list of the best brunch spots across NYC for 2025.

New York City’s dining scene is constantly evolving, but the ritual of weekend brunch remains a constant. From the historic streets of Greenwich Village to the trendy corners of Williamsburg, here are the spots you simply cannot miss.

1. The Breakfast Club in Greenwich Village

This iconic spot has been serving perfect eggs for 30 years. Their fluffy pancakes are a must-try. The atmosphere is cozy, reminiscent of a classic American diner but with a modern culinary twist. Be prepared for a wait, but trust us, it's worth it.

Pro Tip: Order the "Sunrise Skillet" – it's not on the main menu but it's a local favorite.

2. Modern Table in Brooklyn

A modern take on brunch with avocado toast and creative cocktails. Located in the heart of Williamsburg, it attracts a trendy crowd looking for great aesthetics and even better food. Their "Green Goddess" smoothie bowl is as delicious as it is photogenic.

3. La Parisienne (SoHo)

For those who prefer a sweeter start, La Parisienne offers the most authentic French pastries this side of the Atlantic. Their almond croissants flake perfectly, and the café au lait is served in traditional oversized bowls.
