For Diners

Is Food Spot free to use?

Yes! Searching for restaurants, reading reviews, and booking tables is 100% free for all diners.

How do I verify my review?

We take authenticity seriously. To leave a verified review, you must either book a table through our platform (we'll email you a link after your meal) or verify your phone number via SMS.

Can I cancel or modify a reservation?

Absolutely. Go to your Dashboard > Upcoming Reservations. You can modify the guest count or cancel up to 30 minutes before your scheduled time.

How do the coupons work?

Navigate to our 'Deals' page, click 'Get Coupon', and present the digital code to your server before ordering. No printing required!

For Business Owners

How much does it cost to list my restaurant?

We offer a Free Tier which includes a basic listing. Our Premium plans start at $49/mo and include advanced analytics, top search placement, and zero-commission booking tools.

How do I claim an existing listing?

If you see your business already listed, click the "Claim this Business" link on the profile page. You will need to provide proof of ownership via phone or email verification.

Can I respond to reviews?

Yes, all registered business owners can reply to reviews publicly. We encourage professional and constructive responses.

Account & Billing

I forgot my password.

Click "Log In" and select "Forgot Password". We will send a secure reset link to your registered email address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal for Premium subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

We're sad to see you go. You can delete your account from the Settings tab in your user dashboard. This action is permanent.
